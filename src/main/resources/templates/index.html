Description:

  • Application allows you to generate epidemic simulation, based on initial data:
    • Simulation name
    • Population size
    • Number of infected people - number of people who are infected at the beginning of simulation
    • Number of people who will be infected by one person
    • Mortality rate - describes how many of the infected people will die
    • Days from infection to recovery
    • Days from infection to death
    • Number of simulation days
    After calculations you will get data for every simulation day, which contains:
    • Number of infected people
    • Number of healthy people who can be infected
    • Number of deaths
    • Number of people who will be infected by one person
    • Number of people who recovered
