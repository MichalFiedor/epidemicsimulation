Description:

Application allows you to generate epidemic simulation, based on initial data:

  • Simulation name
  • Population size (P)
  • Number of infected people - number of people who are infected at the beginning of simulation
  • Number of people who will be infected by one person (R)
  • Mortality rate - describes how many of the infected people will die (M)
  • Days from infection to recovery (Ti)
  • Days from infection to death (Tm)
  • Number of simulation days (Ts)

After calculations you will get data for every simulation day, which contains:

  • Number of infected people (Pi)
  • Number of healthy people who can be infected (Pv)
  • Number of deaths (Pm)
  • Number of people who will be infected by one person (Pr)
  • Number of people who recovered

Main assumptions:

  • "M" infected people, who infected "Tm" days before, moves form infected people to death people
  • All infected people "Ti" days before (and who is alive) moves from infected people to people who recovered
  • Every infected person infects "R" healthy people who can be infected

As a result for Ts simulation days you will get Ts simulations where Pi + Pv + Pm + Pr = P
